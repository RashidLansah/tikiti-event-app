Facts & Questions

How do I create an account on Tikiti?

+

Creating an account is simple! Download the Tikiti app from the App Store, open it, and tap "Create Account". You can sign up with your email address or use Apple Sign-In for a quick setup. Once registered, you can customize your profile and start discovering events in your area.

How do I RSVP for an event and get my QR ticket?

+

Browse events in the app, tap on one you're interested in, and hit the "RSVP" button. For paid events, complete the payment process. Once confirmed, you'll instantly receive a digital ticket with a unique QR code. This ticket is stored in your "My Tickets" section and works offline - just show it at the event entrance!

How do I upgrade to an organizer account?

+

Go to your Profile tab and tap "Upgrade to Organizer". Choose your subscription plan and complete the payment. Once upgraded, you'll have access to the organizer dashboard where you can create events, manage attendees, scan QR codes, and view detailed analytics for your events.

How do I create and publish an event?

+

As an organizer, tap the "+" button in your dashboard to create a new event. Fill in all the details including title, description, date, time, location, and pricing. Upload photos, set capacity limits, and choose your event category. Once everything looks good, tap "Publish" and your event will go live for attendees to discover and RSVP.

How do I scan QR codes at the event entrance?

+

Open the Tikiti app and go to your organizer dashboard. Tap "Scan Tickets" and point your camera at the attendee's QR code. The app will instantly validate the ticket, check the attendee in, and update your attendance count. You can also manually check in attendees if needed.

How do I check my event data and analytics?

+

In your organizer dashboard, tap on any of your events to view detailed analytics. You'll see real-time attendance numbers, check-in rates, revenue data, attendee demographics, and engagement metrics. Export reports for your records or share insights with your team. All data is updated in real-time as people check in.

Is my personal data and payment information secure?

+

Absolutely! Tikiti uses enterprise-grade security to protect your data. All payment processing is handled by secure, PCI-compliant payment processors. Your personal information is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your data to third parties, and you have full control over your privacy settings.

What devices and operating systems are supported?

+

Tikiti is currently available on iOS devices running iOS 13 or later. We're actively developing an Android version that will be released soon. The app works on all iPhone models from iPhone 8 onwards, and you can also access basic features through our web platform for event RSVPs.
