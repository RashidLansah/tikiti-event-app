‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an event?

Tap the "+" button on the organizer dashboard and follow the event creation flow. You'll need to provide event details, date, time, location, and upload an image.

How do I RSVP to an event?

Browse events on the main screen, tap on an event you're interested in, and use the "RSVP Now" button. You'll receive a digital ticket with a QR code.

How do I scan QR codes for tickets?

As an organizer, use the QR scanner in your dashboard to validate attendee tickets at the event entrance. The scanner will show attendee details when you scan a valid ticket.

How do I download my ticket?

After RSVPing, go to "My Tickets" tab to view and download your digital ticket. You can also share it or save it to your photos.

Can I cancel my RSVP?

Yes, you can cancel your RSVP by going to "My Tickets" and selecting the event you want to cancel. Please note that some events may have cancellation policies.

How do I get notifications about events?

Make sure notifications are enabled in your device settings. You'll receive reminders 1 hour before events and updates from organizers.

Is Tikiti free to use?

Yes! Tikiti is completely free for both event attendees and organizers. There are no hidden fees or subscription costs.

How do I report a problem or bug?

Please email us at gettikiti@gmail.com with "Bug Report" in the subject line. Include details about what happened and your device information.
